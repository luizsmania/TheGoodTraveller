Havana - Cuba

Historically fascinating, culturally rich and visually stunning, Cuba does things in its own way. From the moment you arrive on this Caribbean pearl, every winding street and jaw-dropping beach you discover will astound you even more than the last. If you find yourself drawn to the charm and historical allure of Cuba’s big cities, Havana (the capital of Cuba), Cienfuegos and Santiago de Cuba tick all the boxes. Alternatively, if the waves are calling, you’ll find soft sands and shades of blue you only see in travel brochures in the beach resort of Varadero.
